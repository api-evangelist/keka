The article emphasizes that 86% of new hires decide within their first six months whether to stay with a company, and outlines six essential onboarding checklists: welcome email, pre-onboarding, first day, first week, 30/60/90-day milestones, and remote employee onboarding. It stresses that onboarding should last at least a year with clear task ownership across departments, recommending spreading information over time, pairing new hires with mentors, and centralizing the process in dedicated HR software.
