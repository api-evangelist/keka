The article addresses how inconsistent offboarding costs companies up to $500,000 annually through knowledge loss and security risks, presenting five structured checklists covering HR responsibilities (final pay, benefits termination, exit interviews), manager duties, IT tasks (access revocation, device retrieval), finance functions, and remote-specific considerations. It emphasizes that proper offboarding protects compliance, maintains security, preserves institutional knowledge, and impacts employer reputation.
