Nearly 70% of companies reported payroll data issues in 2024, with over 82 million workers experiencing paycheck errors. The article explains that payroll automation removes manual calculations, tax withholdings, and compliance filings, delivering time savings and 31% fewer errors overall, and details the automated payroll cycle from data collection through payment processing. It compares leading platforms including Keka, ADP, Gusto, and Rippling for different business sizes.
